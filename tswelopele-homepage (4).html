Why 2,400+ Travellers Trust Us

There are many travel agents in South Africa. There is only one Tswelopele. Here is the difference.

🗺️

Hyper-Personalised Journeys

No two itineraries are alike. Every journey begins with a real conversation — not a form — where we listen first and plan second. Your trip is yours, shaped around what excites you, not what fills our quota.

01
🤝

Ubuntu at Our Core

Umuntu ngumuntu ngabantu — a person is a person through other people. Ubuntu is not our tagline; it's how we work. You are not a booking reference. You are a guest in our home country, and we treat you accordingly.

02
🌱

Travel That Gives Back

We partner only with community-owned lodges, ethical operators, and conservation reserves. A portion of every booking supports the communities whose land you are privileged to visit. Your rand does real good here.

03
📍

Born Here. Know Every Corner.

Our founder grew up in Limpopo. Our team lives across SA's provinces. We don't consult guidebooks — we call the local rangers, guesthouse owners, and community leaders directly. The knowledge you get is firsthand and real.

04
🏆

Accredited & Accountable

SATSA-accredited with full compliance to South Africa's tourism regulations and POPIA data protection. Your deposits are secured, your itineraries are insured, and you have a real person to call — not a chatbot — if anything changes.

05
💬

With You Before, During & After

Planning your trip is the beginning, not the end. We're available on WhatsApp throughout your journey, provide detailed digital travel guides beforehand, and send every client a curated "Your Chapter" photo story two weeks after they return home.

A Message From Our Founder

Born From a Deep Love
of This Extraordinary Land

My name is Kgobedi, and I grew up in Limpopo understanding something that I could feel long before I could explain it: South Africa is not just a place on a map. It is a living, breathing story.

As a child, I watched travellers arrive wide-eyed at the edge of the bush and leave transformed — quieter, fuller, changed in ways they couldn't quite name. I knew then that sharing this land was my calling. Not as a commodity. Not as a product. But as an act of genuine hospitality.

"We are not a travel agency that sells South Africa. We are South Africans who invite the world to sit at our table, walk in our footsteps, and carry a piece of our story home."

— Kgobedi, Founder, Tswelopele Travel
